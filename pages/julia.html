Julia Sobchenko Photography

My roles:

UX Designer, UX Researcher, UI Designer

Overview

Julia, a young ukrainian photographer sought to create a visually captivating and accessible online portfolio to showcase her work and attract potential clients that would book her. The website aimed to exhibit a wide range of photographic styles, highlight the photographer's skills, and provide an effortless user experience on both desktop and mobile devices.

Goals

- User-Centered Design: Prioritize user needs and preferences to ensure that the website offers an exceptional user experience for both art enthusiasts and clients.

- Responsive Design: Create a design that is fully responsive, adapting seamlessly to both desktop and mobile devices, to guarantee an optimal viewing experience.

- Artwork Showcase: Create a visually captivating gallery section that beautifully showcases the photographer's artwork, facilitating easy viewing and potential bookings from clients.

- Client Collaboration: Collaborate closely with the photographer to understand their artistic vision and requirements, translating them into effective design solutions.

Design Process

Research Phase

- Conducted user interviews and surveys to understand pain points related to meal planning and cooking.

- Analyzed existing meal planning apps to identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for improvement.

- Optimize the grocery shopping experience by integrating a smart shopping list.

Ideation

- Brainstormed and sketched initial wireframes and app flow based on user feedback and research insights.

- Defined user personas representing different target groups to guide design decisions.

- Explored various design styles and color schemes to create a visually appealing interface.
