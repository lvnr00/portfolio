PirateBlood Studios

My roles:

UX Designer, UX Researcher, UI Designer

Overview

PirateBlood Studios is an innovative game developer studio focused on AR and mobile games. With a desire to showcase their portfolio, connect with gamers, and attract new talent, they embarked on their new website project. As the UX designer for PirateBlood Studios, I played a pivotal role in this project.

Goals

- Showcase Portfolio: Create an engaging platform to showcase PirateBlood Studios' portfolio of games, focusing on visuals, gameplay, and user experiences.

- Community Building: Establish a community hub to connect with gamers, and provide a space for fans to interact.

- Talent Attraction: Improve the studio's talent acquisition efforts by presenting the studio's vision.

Target Audience

The primary target audience for the PirateBlood Studios website includes gamer enthusiasts, individuals passionate about gaming who seek new gaming experiences, updates on PirateBlood Studios' releases, and interaction with other gamers; and game developers, professionals in the gaming industry interested in career opportunities, collaboration, and industry insights.

Design Process

Research Phase

- Conducted user research to understand the needs and preferences of both gamers and game developers.

- Analyzed competitor websites to identify industry best practices.

Ideation

- Brainstormed and sketched initial wireframes and early prototypes for both the desktop and mobile versions.

- Defined user personas representing the different target groups.

- Explored various design styles and color schemes to create a visually appealing interface attractive to gamers.

Testing and Feedback

Key Takeaways

- User-Centric Approach: Prioritizing user needs and preferences in design resulted in increased user engagement and positive feedback.

- Effective Showcase: The interactive portfolio showcase effectively showcased PirateBlood Studios' games, capturing the essence of each game and drawing users in.

- Talent Attraction: A more transparent careers section improved talent acquisition efforts and allowed prospective employees to better understand the studio's culture and values.

- Responsive Design: The responsive design ensured accessibility across devices, catering to both desktop and mobile users.
