MealMaster

My roles:

UX Designer, UX Researcher, UI Designer

Overview

MealMaster is a mobile app designed to simplify and enhance the meal planning experience for users. It aims to address the challenges of meal preparation, grocery shopping, and maintaining a balanced diet by offering personalized meal plans, easy-to-follow recipes, and a smart shopping list.

The app targets health-conscious individuals, busy professionals, and anyone seeking to adopt healthier eating habits.

Problem Statement

Many people struggle with meal planning, leading to unhealthy eating habits, wastage of food, and increased stress in the kitchen. The challenges faced by users include:

- Difficulty in creating balanced meal plans tailored to their dietary preferences and health goals.

- Lack of inspiration for new recipes and variety in their meals.

- Inefficiency in managing shopping lists, leading to multiple store visits and overspending.

Goals

- Create a user-friendly mobile app that streamlines the meal planning process.

- Provide personalized meal plans and recipes based on users' dietary requirements and health goals.

- Optimize the grocery shopping experience by integrating a smart shopping list.

Design Process

Research Phase

- Conducted user interviews and surveys to understand pain points related to meal planning and cooking.

- Analyzed existing meal planning apps to identify strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for improvement.

- Optimize the grocery shopping experience by integrating a smart shopping list.

Ideation

- Brainstormed and sketched initial wireframes and app flow based on user feedback and research insights.

- Defined user personas representing different target groups to guide design decisions.

- Explored various design styles and color schemes to create a visually appealing interface.

Prototyping

- Created interactive prototypes using Figma to demonstrate the app's key features and user flows.

- Conducted usability testing with potential users to gather feedback and identify areas for improvement.

Interactive prototype

Design Solution

- Home Screen: A personalized dashboard presenting the user's current meal plan, calorie count, and recommended recipes.

- Meal Planner: Users can customize their meal plans by selecting dietary preferences, health goals, and desired recipes. The app generates a weekly meal plan accordingly.

- Recipe Library: A diverse collection of healthy recipes with detailed instructions, nutritional information, and user ratings.

- Smart Shopping List: Ingredients from chosen recipes automatically populate the shopping list, organized by aisles for efficient shopping.

- Profile and Settings: Users can manage dietary preferences, allergies, and view their meal history.

User Testing

- Conducted further usability testing with a larger group of users to validate design decisions and identify any remaining issues.

- Iteratively refined the app design based on user feedback, ensuring intuitive navigation and ease of use.

Final Product

The final version of MealMaster presented a polished, user-friendly app that catered to the needs of health-conscious individuals.

Users could effortlessly plan their meals, access nutritious recipes, and efficiently manage their grocery shopping, all within one integrated platform.

Key Takeaways

- In-depth research and user feedback are essential to understanding user pain points and needs.

- Personalization and tailored recommendations greatly enhance the user experience.

- Iterative design and usability testing lead to a more intuitive and user-friendly app.

- Integrating multiple features into a single platform creates a comprehensive solution.
